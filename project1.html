SENSORY INTERACTION

To improve people’s environmental perception

PROJECT OVERVIEW

The landscape recources in Sun Yat-sen University are rich but people are still not satisfied with the current activity places. They also complain about the insufficiency of open space. Design goal here is to enhance people’s perception of the environment in order to provide them a better experience.

SITE RESEARCH

To better understand fundatmental elements of the site, I used layer analysis mehtod.The high vegetation rate shows the site is rich in natural recources. From field survey, I found out two features that should be highlightened in design.
